ASCII Sokoban: Illustrative #18

HINT: One may attempt to solve this level by tak­ing the one box from the cluster with a surplus all the way to the de­fi­cient cluster. However, it is much easier to carry one box from each clus­ter, start­ing with the sec­ond clus­ter, to the pre­vi­ous clus­ter, thus cor­rect­ing the de­fi­cien­cy of the first cluster and the sur­plus of the last cluster.
